About

College Marketplace is a web application that aims to create a virtual marketplace for college students to buy, sell, and trade items. It addresses the challenges students face in managing expenses and finding affordable resources such as textbooks, furniture, and electronics. The goal is to provide a convenient and secure platform where students can connect and engage in transactions within their college community, fostering a sense of community, sustainability, and cost-effectiveness.


This web application uses the following NPM Packages:

  • express
  • mongoose
  • express-session
  • bycript
